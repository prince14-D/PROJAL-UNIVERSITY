Library
Extensive resources and research centers.
Location
Located at the heart of Monrovia, Liberia.

The Leelai M Kpukuyou Library

The Leelai M Kpukuyou Library provides students access to important research resources. The Library also offers online access to opinions of the Institution and the Liberia Code of Law for students reading Criminal Justice.

The Library is open to students six days a week, Monday to Friday, from 8 AM to 8 PM, and Saturday, from 9 AM to 2 PM.

State of the Arts Computer Lab

The State of the Arts Computer Lab is equipped with modern computers and high-speed internet, providing students with a conducive environment to learn and work on various digital projects. The lab is open during working hours and supports a wide range of software for academic and research purposes.

Mother Vivian Landford Student Lounge

The Mother Vivian Landford Student Lounge offers a relaxing space for students to unwind between classes. Equipped with comfortable seating and a peaceful atmosphere, the lounge is perfect for socializing, studying, or enjoying some downtime.
